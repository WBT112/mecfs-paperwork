# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and productive environment for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; ask clarifying questions.
- Focus on what is best for users (privacy-first, offline-first).
- Keep discussions professional and on-topic.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sharing or requesting real patient data or other sensitive personal data.
- Publishing exploit-ready security details in public threads.

## Reporting and enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainer via GitHub (issue or direct message). Reports will be handled as promptly and discreetly as possible.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels).
